About These Values

These values represent community consensus as of March 2026. They are relative values (not Robux prices) — a pet with a value of 110 is roughly equivalent in trade to eleven pets with a value of 10 each.

Demand (↑/→/↓) reflects the current market trend: whether more players are seeking this pet right now. A pet on ↑ trend may trade above its listed value in competitive situations.

Note on Bat Dragon vs Shadow Dragon: The Bat Dragon is widely considered more valuable by the trading community, though the two are close enough that trading one for the other (with small adds) is generally considered fair.
