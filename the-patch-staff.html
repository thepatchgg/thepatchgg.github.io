The Patch Staff

The editorial team behind The Patch's Adopt Me values, update guides, and calculators.

The Patch Staff is the byline used for value pages, weekly update guides, egg coverage, and comparison pieces that are reviewed against the site's methodology before they go live. The goal is simple: clearer guidance, visible corrections, and fewer pages that feel disconnected from one another.

What the byline means

When a page is credited to The Patch Staff, it means the content is being published as an editorial team page rather than a one-off personal post. That matters most on value pages, calculator help, and fast update guides.

How guides get reviewed

The team checks official Adopt Me update notes first for live event details, then uses the site's methodology pages to keep value language and calculator guidance aligned with the broader Patch system.

How corrections work

If a page is wrong or stale, the expectation is to fix it quickly, keep the trust pages aligned, and log meaningful user-facing changes publicly when they affect confidence.
