About The Patch

The Patch is a game analysis publication focused on one thing: getting you accurate, opinionated patch coverage faster than anywhere else.

What we cover

We cover five live-service games on predictable schedules:

  • League of Legends — Every patch, biweekly
  • Valorant — Every patch, biweekly
  • Fortnite — Every update, biweekly
  • Adopt Me! (Roblox) — Every update, weekly on Fridays
  • Marvel Rivals — Every patch, biweekly

Why we exist

Patch notes are long. Meta implications aren't obvious. And most sites either publish 12 hours after a patch drops or pad their analysis with filler to hit a word count. We don't do either.

Our goal is simple: by the time you're in champion select or loading into a match, we've already told you what changed, what it means, and what to play.

The Adopt Me! difference

We're one of the only gaming publications that takes Roblox Adopt Me! as seriously as competitive titles — with dedicated trading value analysis, event guides, and a "For Parents" section in every update. If your kid plays Adopt Me!, this is where you find out what changed, what it costs, and whether the new pet is worth trading for.
