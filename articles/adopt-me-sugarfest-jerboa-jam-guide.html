Quick Week 3 guide

Week 3 reset the pace of Sugarfest with Latte Kitsune, Jiggly Jerboa, and Jerboa Jam, giving the event a fresh late-run grind loop.

What made Week 3 important

Late-event weeks often decide whether players stay invested. Sugarfest Week 3 worked because it added a new premium chase pet while also giving free-to-play players a pet they could realistically collect in volume.

Latte Kitsune attention

Latte Kitsune carried the spotlight because it looked premium and gave traders a new headline pet to talk about after the launch-week excitement had cooled.

Jiggly Jerboa stacks

Jiggly Jerboa mattered because it was easier to target in multiples, which always matters once players start planning Neons before an event ends.

Jerboa Jam loop

Jerboa Jam refreshed the Candy Egg grind and gave the late-event weeks their own identity instead of feeling like leftover launch content.
