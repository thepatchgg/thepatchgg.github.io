🔴 Week 3 is live — March 27, 2026

Sugarfest Week 3 went live March 27 at 11:15am ET. Latte Kitsune is now available for 600 Robux. Earn the Jiggly Jerboa by collecting 6,000 Candy Eggs in the Jerboa Jam minigame. Event ends April 6.

⚡ Week 3 at a Glance

Two new pets land March 27 — Latte Kitsune (Legendary, 600 Robux) and Jiggly Jerboa (Common, 6,000 Candy Eggs). A brand-new minigame called Jerboa Jam launches alongside a new Candy Cliffs area. The event ends April 6, so this is the final week to earn Candy Eggs.

☕ Latte Kitsune — What You Need to Know

The Latte Kitsune is a Legendary pet available exclusively through the Robux shop for 600 Robux (~$7.50). It joined the shop on March 27 alongside the Week 3 update.

💰 Trade Value Callout: Early estimates put Latte Kitsune at ~500 Normal / ~1,500 FR. As a Legendary Robux pet, expect it to hold decent value. Demand is strong right now but will cool after the event closes April 6.

🐹 Jiggly Jerboa — Earning Through Jerboa Jam

The Jiggly Jerboa is a Common pet earned by collecting 6,000 Candy Eggs. The fastest way to earn Candy Eggs is through the new Jerboa Jam minigame in the Candy Cliffs area.

Jerboa Jam Minigame — How It Works

  1. Head to the Candy Cliffs area (look for the new map marker)
  2. Enter the Jerboa Jam minigame portal
  3. Collect as many Candy Eggs as possible during each round
  4. Repeat until you hit 6,000 Candy Eggs to unlock the Jiggly Jerboa

💡 Tip: The Jiggly Jerboa is Common rarity, so making a Neon only requires 4 pets (vs. 16 for Legendary). With strong Neon potential, grinding multiple Jerboa is a smart play before the event ends.
