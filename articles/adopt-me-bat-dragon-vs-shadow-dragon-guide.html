Bat Dragon vs Shadow Dragon: which high-tier anchor is stronger right now?

These two pets still define the top of a lot of Adopt Me trade conversations. The real question is not just which one is rarer in theory, but which one is stronger in the lane you are actually trading: default, no-potion, neon, or mega.

Bat Dragon default: 578 Shadow Dragon default: 372 Reviewed April 2026

Quick verdict

Bat Dragon is still the stronger everyday anchor in the default lane, which is why it sits ahead on The Patch benchmark ladder. Shadow Dragon is still a monster, but it is the no-potion premium and collector pressure that make it uniquely dangerous to underestimate.

Where Bat Dragon wins

  • Default lane: Bat Dragon leads by about 206 points, which is why it still anchors more expensive everyday trade discussions.
  • Neon and mega ladder: Bat Dragon stays comfortably ahead in the finished neon and mega lanes too, so the overall hierarchy still holds.
  • Cleaner trading conversations: Bat Dragon is easier to price quickly because the default lane already reflects most of its strength.

Where Shadow Dragon fights back

  • No-potion premium: Shadow Dragon jumps from 372 to 502 in the no-potion lane, which is a much bigger collector swing than Bat Dragon gets.
  • Collector positioning: Shadow Dragon clean copies are one of the easiest ways for a trade to get more expensive than it first looks.
  • Mega no-potion signal: The mega no-potion lane is still enormous, which tells you how serious the collector market is here.

How to trade this matchup

If you are comparing default copies, Bat Dragon is the stronger anchor. If a trade involves clean Shadow Dragon copies, slow down before calling it fair, because that is where the pricing gap tightens fastest and server intuition breaks down.
