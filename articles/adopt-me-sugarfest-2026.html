Adopt Me! Sugarfest 2026: All New Pets, Values & Complete Event Guide

March 13, 2026· Updated same day as patch· Event ends April 10, 2026

TL;DR — The big takeaways

  1. 4 new pets — one Legendary (paid), one Ultra Rare, one Rare, one Uncommon.
  2. Board game is the core loop — Roll Sugar Dice in Game of Sweets to earn event currencies.
  3. 4-week event with weekly drops — New items added every Friday through April 10. Don't rush trades early.

New Pets — Full Breakdown

Candicorn
Legendary
S-Tier Value
How to get:1,000 Robux (~$12.50) from the in-game shop Availability:Event-limited — gone after April 10 Early demand:Very High

Robux-gated supply is the key factor. Not every player can or will spend real money, permanently capping how many Candicorns enter the economy. Add Legendary rarity and an event-only window and you have a strong long-term hold.

If you're investing, this is the one. Buy low in Week 3–4 when hype fades. Value increases after event ends.

Gumball Caterpillar
Ultra Rare
A-Tier (Week 1)
How to get:Collect 5 Gumballs via Game of Sweets board game Availability:Event-limited Early demand:High (dropping by Week 3)

Ultra Rare rarity with a grind gate makes this the best free pet in the event. Players who complete it in Week 1 have a trading advantage. Supply will increase significantly as the event progresses.

Trade your early Caterpillar for a non-Sugarfest Legendary now. Value will drop ~30–40% by Week 3.

Gummy Guana
Rare
B-Tier Value
How to get:Use 7 Candy Chisels at Candy Cliffs Availability:Event-limited

Rare rarity limits the ceiling, but the Candy Chisel requirement adds a modest grind gate. Solid collector pet that will hold modest value post-event.

Worth getting for your collection. Post-event nostalgia value is real for event-limited Rares.

Cocoadile
Uncommon
C-Tier Value
How to get:14,000 Candy Eggs at the Cocoa Campfire (night only) Availability:Event-limited

Uncommon rarity caps trading potential. Active players will accumulate Candy Eggs quickly. Expect this pet to be widely owned within 2 weeks.

Collect it for completion, but don't trade valuable pets to get one.

Event Activities Guide

🎲 Game of Sweets — Your Primary Loop

Roll Sugar Dice to move around the board and land on reward spaces. Prizes include Candy Eggs, Sugar Dice, Candy Chisels, and Bucks. This is your main path to the Gumball Caterpillar (5 Gumballs needed).

🌊 Cocoa Float

A river-run activity that earns Candy Eggs. Farm 14,000 to unlock the Cocoadile at the Cocoa Campfire at night.

⛏️ Candy Cliffs

Use 7 Candy Chisels (earned from the board game) to unlock the Gummy Guana.

Trading Impact — Week 1

Values Going Up ↑

  • Candicorn: Robux gate + Legendary + limited = long-term hold.
  • Gumball Caterpillar (this week): First-week scarcity gives early grinders trade leverage.

Values Going Down ↓

  • Cocoadile: Will saturate by Week 2. Supply will be high quickly.
  • Non-event Rares: Attention shifts to Sugarfest during events.
