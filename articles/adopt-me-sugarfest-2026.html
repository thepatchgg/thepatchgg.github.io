Adopt Me! Sugarfest 2026: All New Pets, Values & Complete Event Guide

March 13, 2026 · Updated same day as patch · Event ends April 10, 2026

TL;DR — The big takeaways

  1. 4 new pets — one Legendary (paid), one Ultra Rare, one Rare, one Uncommon. Each unlocked differently.
  2. Board game is the core loop — Roll Sugar Dice in Game of Sweets to earn event currencies and progress toward free pets.
  3. 4-week event with weekly drops — New items added every Friday through April 10. Don't rush trades early.

New Pets — Full Breakdown

Candicorn
Legendary
S-Tier Value
How to get: 1,000 Robux (~$12.50) from the in-game shop Availability: Event-limited — gone after April 10 Early demand: Very High

Robux-gated supply is the key factor here. Not every player can or will spend real money, which permanently caps how many Candicorns enter the economy. Add Legendary rarity and an event-only window and you have a strong long-term hold. The unicorn theme also has broad appeal across age groups.

If you're investing, this is the one. Buy low in Week 3–4 when hype fades. Value increases after event ends.

Gumball Caterpillar
Ultra Rare
A-Tier (Week 1)
How to get: Collect 5 Gumballs via Game of Sweets board game Availability: Event-limited Early demand: High (dropping by Week 3)

Ultra Rare rarity with a grind gate makes this the best free pet in the event. The board game mechanic takes real time — players who complete it in Week 1 have a trading advantage they won't have in Week 3 when most active players finish the grind. Supply will increase significantly as the event progresses.

Trade your early Caterpillar for a non-Sugarfest Legendary now. Value will drop ~30–40% by Week 3 as supply increases.

Gummy Guana
Rare
B-Tier Value
How to get: Use 7 Candy Chisels at Candy Cliffs Availability: Event-limited Early demand: Medium

Rare rarity limits the ceiling, but the Candy Chisel requirement adds a modest grind gate — you earn Chisels from the board game, so it feeds into the same loop as the Caterpillar. The iguana-style design is niche but has dedicated fans. Solid collector pet that will hold modest value post-event.

Worth getting for your collection. Don't expect high-value trades, but post-event nostalgia value is real for event-limited Rares.

Cocoadile
Uncommon
C-Tier Value
How to get: 14,000 Candy Eggs at the Cocoa Campfire (night only) Availability: Event-limited Early demand: Low–Medium

Uncommon rarity caps trading potential significantly. The night-time restriction is flavourful but won't meaningfully limit supply — active players will accumulate Candy Eggs quickly through the board game loop. Expect this pet to be widely owned within 2 weeks.

Collect it for completion, but don't trade valuable pets to get one. Supply will be high by end of Week 2.

Event Activities Guide

Mayor Amanda hosts a candy-themed world with three interconnected activities. Understanding how they connect is key to getting the free pets efficiently.

🎲 Game of Sweets (Board Game) — Your Primary Loop

Roll Sugar Dice to move around the board and land on reward spaces. Prizes include Candy Eggs, Sugar Dice, Custom Caramel Dice, Bucks, and Candy Chisels. This is your main path to the Gumball Caterpillar (5 Gumballs needed) and feeds into the Gummy Guana unlock.

Tip: Prioritize activities that earn more Sugar Dice. Every roll is a reward — the board is your economy.

🌊 Cocoa Float (River Run)

A river-run activity that earns Candy Eggs. Your path to the Cocoadile — farm 14,000 Candy Eggs to unlock it at the Cocoa Campfire. Play this at night in-game for the Cocoadile unlock.

⛏️ Candy Cliffs (Excavation)

Use Candy Chisels earned from the board game to excavate here. After 7 Chisels used, the Gummy Guana is yours. This is passive — collect Chisels during normal board game play and visit the Cliffs to cash them in.

Trading Impact — Week 1 Analysis

Values Going Up ↑

  • Candicorn: Robux gate + Legendary + limited = long-term hold. Strongest buy in this event.
  • Gumball Caterpillar (this week only): First-week scarcity gives early grinders trade leverage. Window closes by Week 3.
  • Previous event Legendaries: Spring event hype renews general collector interest.

Values Going Down ↓

  • Cocoadile: Will saturate by Week 2. Uncommon + accessible grind = everyone has one quickly.
  • Non-event Rares: Attention shifts to Sugarfest. Generic Rares lose trading momentum during events.

🔥 Trade of the Week

If you have: An early Gumball Caterpillar
Try to get: A non-Sugarfest Legendary that's been declining
Why: The Caterpillar's value peaks right now in Week 1 when supply is scarce. By Week 3, most active players will have completed the board game grind. Trade your first-mover advantage for durable value before the window closes.
