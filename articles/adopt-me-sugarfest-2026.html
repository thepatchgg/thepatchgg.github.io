Sugarfest at a glance

Sugarfest rolled out across four weekly waves, starting with Candicorn and ending with the Chocolate Bunny Box, Pupcake, and Easter Bunny finale.

Why Sugarfest was such a big event

Sugarfest had the exact mix that usually sticks with Adopt Me players: a strong launch lineup, a weekly update rhythm, a clear event loop, and one more memorable closeout wave at the end. That made it both a good play event and a good archive event.

The launch week brought Candicorn, Gumball Caterpillar, Gummy Guana, and Cocoadile. Later weeks layered in Waffle Scuffle, Jerboa Jam, and the Week 4 finale with the Chocolate Bunny Box, Pupcake, and Easter Bunny. Because each Friday added a new mini-wave, players still search these guides long after the event finished.
