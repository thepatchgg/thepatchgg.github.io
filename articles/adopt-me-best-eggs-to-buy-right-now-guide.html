Best eggs to buy right now

If you want the clean premium answer, Crystal Egg is the best ticket egg to buy right now. If you want cheaper volume and a broader pool, Basic Egg is still the best budget hatch on the board.

Reviewed by The Patch Staff using the current Patch egg directory and ticket-egg coverage.
Best premium buy: Crystal Egg Best budget buy: Basic Egg Both are live ticket eggs

Quick verdict

Crystal Egg is the best choice when you are saving for stronger premium odds. Basic Egg is the best choice when you want cheaper openings, more total rolls, or broader collection coverage.

How to use this page

Use this guide when you want a simple answer before opening the full egg pages. It is built for players who just want to know where their next tickets should go.

Why Crystal Egg is the premium winner

  • Better top-end odds: Crystal Egg has a much stronger legendary lane than Basic Egg.
  • No low-end clutter: It avoids the common-heavy feel that makes cheaper eggs less efficient for premium hunting.
  • Cleaner value chase: If you are saving up for rarer pulls, Crystal Egg is the more focused use of tickets.

Why Basic Egg still deserves a spot

  • Budget-friendly: It lets you open much more often without waiting on a huge ticket stack.
  • Broader hatch pool: It is still a strong collection egg when your goal is variety instead of pure premium odds.
  • Lower-risk sessions: You can absorb bad runs much more easily because each opening is so cheap.

Buy Crystal Egg if...

You are saving tickets on purpose, chasing stronger hatches, and do not want your openings diluted by commons. This is the right call for premium-focused sessions.
