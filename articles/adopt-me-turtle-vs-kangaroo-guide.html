Turtle vs Kangaroo: which one is the stronger everyday trade target?

This is one of the most common practical trade debates because both pets are familiar, both came from the same era, and both still show up constantly in mid-high offers. Turtle is the stronger hold on the benchmark ladder, while Kangaroo remains the more reachable step for players climbing into that lane.

Reviewed by The Patch Staff using the current Patch benchmark ladder and public methodology pages.
Turtle default: 23.25 Kangaroo default: 17 Common upgrade debate

Quick verdict

Turtle is the stronger destination in straight value terms and usually the safer answer if you are choosing one pet to hold. Kangaroo still matters because it is easier to buy into and can work as a practical stepping stone toward Turtle-tier offers.

Best use of this page

Use this comparison when someone acts like the two pets are basically the same. They are close enough to be grouped together in player memory, but not close enough to ignore the value gap.

Why Turtle leads

  • Stronger default lane: Turtle leads by about 6.25 points, which is a meaningful gap in this part of the market.
  • Better demand profile: Turtle keeps stronger everyday demand, which is why traders so often treat it as the better final destination.
  • Higher finished variants: Turtle stays ahead in neon and mega too, so the lead is consistent across upgraded copies.

Why Kangaroo still stays relevant

  • Cheaper way in: Kangaroo is easier to reach, so it stays useful for players building up toward stronger mid-high pets.
  • Shared nostalgia lane: Because it came from the same egg era, many players instinctively compare it to Turtle first.
  • Still respectable: Even though it sits lower, Kangaroo is not a throwaway add pet. It still holds recognizable trade weight.

How to trade this matchup

If the goal is to land in the stronger hold, Turtle is the better answer. If you are building up from smaller pets and need a realistic step before Turtle pricing, Kangaroo can still be the practical route.
