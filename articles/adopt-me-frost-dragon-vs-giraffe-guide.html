Frost Dragon vs Giraffe: which elite pet is stronger right now?

Both of these pets sit in the upper tier of serious Adopt Me offers, but they do different jobs. Giraffe is the stronger raw-value hold, while Frost Dragon is one of the easiest elite anchors to move when you want the next trade quickly.

Reviewed by The Patch Staff using the current Patch benchmark ladder and public methodology pages.
Giraffe default: 277 Frost Dragon default: 206 Both elite demand

Quick verdict

Giraffe is the stronger pure-value destination, and that gap is large enough to matter in real offers. Frost Dragon still matters because it is one of the cleanest high-tier anchors in the game, so the choice often comes down to raw ladder strength versus trade flexibility.

Best use of this page

Use this comparison when a trade feels close because both pets are popular. The gap is real, but the server feel can get muddy because Frost Dragon moves so often and Giraffe gets treated more like a prestige hold.

Why Giraffe wins on paper

  • Stronger default lane: Giraffe leads by about 71 points, which is too large to wave away as normal server variance.
  • Bigger finished variants: Giraffe also stays clearly ahead in the neon and mega lanes, so the hierarchy is not just a single default-lane quirk.
  • Collector strength: Giraffe keeps a prestige edge that shows up most clearly when traders want to park value in a pet they can hold confidently.

Why Frost Dragon still gets picked

  • Cleaner trade movement: Frost Dragon is one of the easiest elite pets to price fast and move again in active servers.
  • More common anchor use: Traders often build around Frost Dragon by instinct, which makes it feel easier to slot into next-step offers.
  • Still a top pet: Even though Giraffe is higher, Frost Dragon is not some budget fallback. It still sits firmly in the elite lane.

How to trade this matchup

If the choice is just raw ladder strength, Giraffe is the stronger target. If you know you want to move the pet again soon, Frost Dragon can still be the more comfortable hold because it stays so liquid in high-tier trade rooms.
