Adopt Me Pet Value List — March 2026 (Updated)

March 13, 2026 · Sugarfest 2026 active · Post-Lunar New Year market

TL;DR — March 2026 market in three bullets

  1. Shadow Dragon is still the #1 most valuable tradeable pet — consistently tops every trade server's demand list. No single new pet has come close to its demand since it left the Halloween event rotation.
  2. Lunar Serpent value has cooled post-Chinese New Year — it peaked during the CNY event and is now settling into mid-high tier. Good time to hold, bad time to overpay for one.
  3. Sugarfest 2026 pets are in active circulation — the Candy Dragon and Sugar Glider are the two standout Sugarfest pets trading above their fgg cost right now. Lock them in before the event ends.

How to Use This Value List

Pet values in Adopt Me are demand-driven — a pet is worth exactly what someone will give you for it. This list reflects current trading community consensus across major Adopt Me trade servers and represents fair trade value, not what scammers will try to get from you. Values are given as approximate demand tiers since there's no official exchange rate. When in doubt, always check the most recent trades in the main trade server before accepting an offer.

One important note on Neon and Mega-Neon pets: a Neon version of a pet is generally worth 4× the normal version (since it takes 4 normal pets to make a Neon). A Mega-Neon is worth roughly 4× a Neon. These multipliers hold fairly consistently across almost every pet on this list.

March 2026 Trading Tips

Good Trades Right Now

  • Trade Neon Commons for Legendaries — Neon Common pets (especially Neon Cats, Neon Dogs, Neon Robins) have consistent demand from players who don't want to farm them. 3–4 Neon Commons can often get you a standard legendary.
  • Stack adds when offering for A-tier pets — When trying to trade for Owl, Parrot, Kangaroo, or Turtle, always have a clear add or two ready. These pets are in high demand and their holders know it. A legendary alone isn't enough — a legendary + two adds makes your offer visible.
  • Get Sugarfest legendaries now — See the section above. The post-event price spike on Candy Dragon and Sugar Glider is historically predictable.

Trades to Avoid Right Now

  • Don't overpay for Lunar Serpent post-CNY — Some traders are still trying to sell Lunar Serpents at their event-peak prices. Market value has come down. Check recent completed trades before offering event-premium prices.
  • Don't trade Shadow Dragon or Bat Dragon for single offers — No single non-S+ pet is worth either of these. If someone is asking for your Shadow Dragon for a single Owl, that's a bad trade. You should be getting an Owl plus multiple adds, or stacking 3–4 A-tier pets minimum.
  • Don't value Neons equally across all pets — A Neon Shadow Dragon is worth dramatically more than 4× Shadow Dragon because it's essentially impossible to obtain (you'd need 4 Shadow Dragons to make it). The Neon multiplier is variable, not fixed.

Scam Awareness

The most common Adopt Me scams in current trade servers: fake trust trades ("trade me first then I'll trade back"), overhyped seasonal pet values ("my Candy Bee is worth a Dragon because it's Sugarfest"), and cross-game scams where traders claim real-money equivalents that aren't valid. Always use the in-game trade window, never trade outside of it, and verify pet values in at least two trade servers before finalizing any high-value exchange.
