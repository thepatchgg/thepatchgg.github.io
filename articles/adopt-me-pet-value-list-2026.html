Understanding Trade Value — FAQs

Fly and Ride potions are consumed when used — they can never be removed or traded. This makes FR pets permanently more useful (they can be ridden like a vehicle AND fly), and the scarcity of the potions themselves adds a supply cost. For lower-rarity pets, a Fly Potion alone is worth more than the pet itself, which is why FR Commons and Uncommons are valued so highly relative to their Normal counterparts.
It depends on patience. A Neon Shadow Dragon requires 4 Shadow Dragons at full-grown age — that's 4× 9,500 = 38,000 in Normal value, but a Neon Shadow Dragon FR is worth ~110,000+. The creation process adds real value because most people can't accumulate 4 Shadow Dragons. If you have 4 Shadow Dragons, making a Neon is almost always worth it unless you need to trade quickly.
The safest long-term holds in March 2026 are: Giraffe FR (limited, 2020, nobody is getting more), Owl FR (from the Farm Egg which is removed from the pool), Axolotl FR (rising cultural interest), and Candicorn FR (just released, supply will freeze after April 10). Avoid holding large quantities of Dragon or Unicorn — their supply never ends.
The classic ladder: start by getting FR Commons from free eggs, trade them for FR Uncommons, trade those for FR Rares, and keep working up. The biggest multiplier is applying FR potions to pets that don't have them yet — find someone with a Normal Rare pet and a Ride potion separately, and trade smarter. Patience is the real currency.

📋 For Parents

Trade values in Adopt Me! are set entirely by player supply and demand — there's no official price list from the developers. The values on this page reflect community consensus as of March 2026. No real money changes hands in trades (only in-game pets), but Robux purchases (like the Candicorn for 1,000 Robux / ~$12.50) do require real money. Always review any Robux spending with your child before purchase.
