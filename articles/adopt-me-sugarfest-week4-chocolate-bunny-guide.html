Week 4 is live — April 3, 2026

The Sugarfest Week 4 update dropped today at 11:15am ET. The Chocolate Bunny Box, Pupcake, and Cake Slice Stroller are all available now. The Easter Bunny Legendary arrives April 4, 2026.

What’s New in Week 4

Week 4 is the biggest pet drop of the entire Sugarfest event, bringing five new pets across multiple price points. The centerpiece is the Chocolate Bunny Box — a 15,000 Candy Egg gift that contains all three Choco Bunny variants at once. Alongside it, the Pupcake arrives as one of the most affordable Robux releases in recent memory at just 99 Robux, and the Easter Bunny Legendary drops live now on April 4.

The event ends April 13, giving you about 10 days to collect everything. After the event a wagon carrying leftover items remains until April 17 if you have Candy Eggs to spend.

Chocolate Bunny Box — How It Works

The Chocolate Bunny Box is a gift item costing 15,000 Candy Eggs that delivers all three Choco Bunny pets in a single purchase — no randomness. Here’s what’s inside:

  • Milk Choco Bunny (Rare) — The base bundle pet. Solid for Neon builds.
  • White Choco Bunny (Ultra-Rare) — Mid-tier. Good limited trade value.
  • Dark Choco Bunny (Legendary) — The jewel of the box. Limited Legendary means strong post-event value.
💡
Fastest way to farm 15,000 Candy Eggs

Stack Waffle Scuffle (up to 5,000 eggs/run) and Jerboa Jam (up to 5,120 eggs/run) — both run on the same 20-minute timer. Alternate between queues and hit 15,000 in as few as 3–4 sessions.

1
Farm Candy Eggs

Waffle Scuffle and Jerboa Jam are the fastest sources. The Cocoa River gives passive eggs too — grab them whenever you pass through.

2
Buy the Chocolate Bunny Box

Find it in the Week 4 event shop for 15,000 Candy Eggs. It’s a gift item, not a random box.

3
Open your gift

Open from your inventory — all three pets (Milk, White, and Dark Choco Bunny) are added to your collection at once.

Pupcake (99 Robux) — Worth It?

At 99 Robux (~$1.25), the Pupcake is one of the cheapest event Robux pets in recent Adopt Me history. Last week’s Latte Kitsune Legendary cost 600 Robux by comparison. Limited Ultra-Rares with low Robux prices tend to be popular and hold decent demand post-event. Whether you’re collecting, trading, or Neon farming, it’s worth grabbing before April 13.

📊
Trade value outlook

Low Robux price = high adoption = more competition in trades, but also strong Neon demand. Expect solid but not explosive post-event value. Still one of the best buys of Sugarfest.

Easter Bunny — Coming April 4

The Easter Bunny is a Legendary released April 4, 2026 — obtain one by hatching every 25 eggs during Admin Abuse! Given it’s a Legendary, expect either a large Candy Egg cost or a Robux option. You’ll have until April 13 to grab it.

⚠️
Only 9 days from release to expiry

Easter Bunny drops April 4 and the event ends April 13. Log in first thing live now to see pricing and plan your grind.

Also New: Cake Slice Stroller

Week 4 also adds the Cake Slice Stroller to the event shop. It is listed alongside the other Week 4 items in the Candy Egg store.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get the Chocolate Bunny Box?
Buy it for 15,000 Candy Eggs in the Week 4 event shop (live April 3). It contains all three Choco Bunnies: Milk (Rare), White (Ultra-Rare), and Dark (Legendary).
Is the Pupcake worth 99 Robux?
Yes — at 99 Robux it’s among the best-value Robux pets in recent event history. Limited Ultra-Rares hold trade value, and the low price makes it easy to justify.
When does the Easter Bunny release in Adopt Me?
April 4, 2026. Obtained by hatching every 25 eggs during the Admin Abuse event. It’s available until the event ends April 13.
How many Candy Eggs do I need for the Chocolate Bunny Box?
15,000 Candy Eggs. Stack Waffle Scuffle and Jerboa Jam minigames — each can pay up to 5,000–5,120 eggs per 20-minute run.
When does Sugarfest end?
April 13, 2026. A wagon stays until April 17 for leftover Candy Eggs, but no new purchases after the 13th.
