Quick Week 4 guide

Week 4 was the Sugarfest finale, bringing the Chocolate Bunny Box, Pupcake, and Easter Bunny together in the event's biggest closing wave.

What landed in Week 4

Week 4 gave Sugarfest a real finale. It combined a themed box, a low-cost premium pet, and one last Legendary target before the event wrapped. That mix made it one of the most memorable weekly update pages from the whole event run.
