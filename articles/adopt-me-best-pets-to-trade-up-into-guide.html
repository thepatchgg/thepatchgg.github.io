Best pets to trade up into right now

The best trade-up target is not always the flashiest pet in the server. It is the one that gives you a better hold, better demand, and a cleaner next step after this trade is over.

Reviewed by The Patch Staff using the current Patch benchmark ladder and public methodology pages.
Turtle: 23.25 Cow: 27 Frost Dragon: 206

Quick verdict

If you are trying to trade up efficiently, target pets that still have strong demand after the trade window closes. Right now, that usually means aiming for Turtle and Cow in the lower premium lane, Evil Unicorn in the upper-middle lane, and Frost Dragon in the elite lane.

How to use this page

Use this guide when you have adds to work with and want a better long-term hold instead of whatever flashy pet happens to be trending in one server.

Best lower-premium targets

  • Turtle: One of the cleanest practical trade-up targets because demand is strong, the lane is familiar, and it stays easy to compare against other mid-high pets.
  • Cow: Still one of the stickiest demand pets in this range, which is why players keep paying attention to it even when the raw ladder gets noisy.

Best upper-mid target

  • Evil Unicorn: A strong next step when you want older demand and recognizable status without paying Crow pricing.
  • Why it works: It stays easy to understand, easy to compare, and easier to move again than a lot of similarly priced niche pets.

Best elite target

  • Frost Dragon: One of the best true trade-up destinations because it combines elite demand with very strong liquidity.
  • Why not always Giraffe: Giraffe is stronger on raw value, but Frost Dragon is often easier to move and build around in real offers.

What to avoid

  • Hard-to-move niche pets: A trade can look good on paper and still stall your progress if the pet is awkward to flip.
  • Server hype only: If a target only feels good because everyone in one server is shouting about it, slow down and re-check the benchmark lane.
