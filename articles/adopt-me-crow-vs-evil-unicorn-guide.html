Crow vs Evil Unicorn: which classic Halloween pet is stronger?

These two pets live in the same collector conversation all the time because both have real demand and recognizable trade history. Crow is the stronger hold on raw value, while Evil Unicorn stays relevant because it is one of the friendlier ways to step into premium Halloween trading.

Reviewed by The Patch Staff using the current Patch benchmark ladder and public methodology pages.
Crow default: 117 Evil Unicorn default: 89 Both older-demand pets

Quick verdict

Crow is the stronger destination on the benchmark ladder and keeps the cleaner prestige edge. Evil Unicorn still matters because it offers strong demand at a lower entry point, which makes it one of the most common upgrade targets for players climbing toward the upper lane.

Best use of this page

Use this comparison when a trade looks close because both pets feel premium. The values are not identical, but they are similar enough that players constantly treat them like they belong in the same family of offers.

Why Crow leads

  • Stronger raw lane: Crow leads by about 28 points in the default lane, which is enough to change what fair add-ons look like.
  • Finished variants stay ahead: Crow also leads in neon and mega, so the advantage holds up across upgraded copies too.
  • Prestige edge: Crow keeps slightly more collector gravity in upper-tier offers, especially when traders are choosing which older Halloween pet to anchor around.

Why Evil Unicorn still gets picked

  • More reachable premium target: Evil Unicorn is easier to step into than Crow, which makes it a very common upgrade destination.
  • Strong demand floor: It still has stable demand and never feels like filler in a serious Halloween trade conversation.
  • No-potion interest: The no-potion lane gets a meaningful bump, which helps cleaner copies stay interesting to collectors.

How to trade this matchup

If you are choosing between the two on pure value strength, Crow is the stronger landing spot. If you are trying to move into strong older demand without paying Crow-level adds, Evil Unicorn is the better budget-conscious target.
