Adopt Me Basic Egg guide: pets, odds, and what is actually worth chasing.

The Basic Egg is the cheapest ticket egg to open in the current rotation, so it gets a lot of attention from players trying to stretch their tickets. This page gives you the full Basic Egg hatch list, the rarity odds behind it, and the standout pets to watch before you spend.

The Basic Egg spreads its odds across a very large hatch pool. That makes it a good collection egg, but not always the best ticket sink if you are only chasing premium pulls.

What makes the Basic Egg useful?

It is the low-cost ticket option, so it is the easiest way to keep hatching without waiting to save for the Crystal Egg. If you are filling out a collection or hoping to snag a long-shot legendary, it stays relevant.

What is the tradeoff?

The top-end odds are tiny. You get volume and variety here, but the premium hits are far less concentrated than they are in the Crystal Egg.
