Quick Week 2 guide

Week 2 gave Sugarfest its second act with Mochi Meow, Waffle Wyrm, and Waffle Scuffle, turning the event into a real weekly rollout.

What made Week 2 important

Week 2 mattered because it turned Sugarfest into a weekly event instead of a one-week novelty. It introduced a new premium chase pet, a second collectible, and a new minigame loop that kept the event moving forward.

Mochi Meow spotlight

Mochi Meow gave Week 2 its headline pet and quickly became one of the most memorable Sugarfest additions because it felt like a true mid-event reward rather than filler.

Waffle Wyrm demand

Waffle Wyrm mattered because it had a strong look and sat in a range many players could realistically aim for, which kept the second-week shop relevant.

Waffle Scuffle loop

Waffle Scuffle helped refresh the event routine. Even players who were not chasing the premium pets still had a new reason to keep grinding.
