ð¥ Waffle Scuffle Minigame â Full Guide

Waffle Scuffle is a PvP knock-off minigame reminiscent of classic Roblox knockback games. You slide pats of butter across a giant waffle board to send opponents flying off the edge â and earn Candy Eggs along the way.

How to Access It

The minigame runs on a 10-minute cycle at the Waffle Circus. Walk up to the entry point and wait for the next match to start. Matches happen automatically â you'll be teleported in when the timer hits zero.

How to Play

1
Sit on Your Butter Cube

When teleported in, your avatar sits atop a butter cube on the waffle platform. A power meter appears at the bottom of your screen.

2
Charge and Launch

Hold your action button to charge the power meter. Release at full power for maximum distance. Aim your butter pad at other players or Fruit Owls.

3
Knock Players Off the Board

Eliminating players and Fruit Owls earns you Candy Eggs. Candies also spawn on the board during the match â skate over them to collect bonus eggs.

4
Collect Your Earnings

Your Candy Egg total is tallied at the end of the match. Higher eliminations = bigger payout. Eggs are added to your pile on the riverbank.

Waffle Scuffle Tips & Strategies

  • Target Fruit Owls first â they don't fight back and give free Candy Eggs. Prioritize them when multiple targets are available.
  • Use the edges â hit players who are already near the edge of the board. Even a low-power shot will knock them off if they're close to the rim.
  • Full-power shots are riskier â a max-power miss can send your butter cube sliding off the edge. Charge to about 70â80% for better aim control.
  • Collect mid-board candies â don't ignore the candy spawns. They can add hundreds of Candy Eggs per match.
  • Play every 10 minutes â the cycle resets frequently. Stack runs during dedicated grinding sessions to maximize Candy Egg income.
