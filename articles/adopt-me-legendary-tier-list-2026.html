Adopt Me Legendary Pets Tier List 2026

Every legendary ranked by trade value, demand, and long-term hold potential — with "worth it?" verdicts for each.

Updated March 21, 2026·12 min read·The Patch Staff
How we rank: Tiers are based on three factors equally weighted — current trade value (what you can realistically get for it), demand (how easy it is to find a buyer), and supply scarcity (whether more can ever enter the game). An S-tier pet is rare, in high demand, and worth a lot. A D-tier pet may be technically "legendary" but is easy to obtain and hard to trade.

All verdicts assume you have the FR version of the pet. Normal (no potions) versions are generally 1 tier lower in practice.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best legendary pet in Adopt Me?
The Shadow Dragon is the best legendary — highest value, never re-released, and in perpetually strong demand since 2019. An FR Shadow Dragon is the #1 trade target for serious players.
What legendary pets can you still get in 2026?
Currently obtainable: Dragon and Unicorn (from Cracked/Pet Eggs), Griffin (requires a GamePass), Diamond versions of Dragon/Unicorn/Griffin (from the Golden Egg at 3% each), and Candicorn during the Sugarfest event until April 10, 2026. Everything else requires trading.
What's the rarest legendary in Adopt Me?
The Shadow Dragon and Bat Dragon. Both were available for purchase during the 2019 Halloween event only — no re-releases in 7 years. The Mega Neon Shadow Dragon is likely the single rarest pet in the game.
How many Unicorns is a Shadow Dragon worth?
An FR Shadow Dragon is worth roughly 75–85 FR Unicorns, or ~350–400 Normal Unicorns. These ratios stay relatively stable over time as both pets' values move together in the overall market.
Is a Giraffe worth more than a Frost Dragon?
Yes, in 2026 an FR Giraffe (~16,000) is worth more than an FR Frost Dragon (~14,000). This wasn't always the case — the Giraffe has been appreciating faster over the last 18 months due to its egg origin story and strong collector demand. The gap may widen further.

📋 For Parents

All legendaries on this list are in-game pets in Adopt Me! on Roblox. "Trading" involves swapping pets between players, not real money. However, some legendaries were originally purchased with Robux (like the Candicorn for ~$12.50 during Sugarfest 2026). No trading in Adopt Me! involves real money transfers — only Robux purchases through Roblox's official store. There are no "trade hacks" that require downloading software; anything claiming otherwise is a scam.
