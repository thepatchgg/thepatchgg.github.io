Sugarfest Week 4 went live April 4 at 11:15am ET. Chocolate Bunny is available for 800 Robux. Pupcake costs 50,000 Candy Eggs. Easter Bunny arrives April 5 via event quests. Event ends April 10.

⚡ Week 4 at a Glance

Three new pets land in the final week of Sugarfest 2026 — Chocolate Bunny (Legendary, 800 Robux), Pupcake (Ultra-Rare, 50K Candy Eggs), and Easter Bunny (Legendary, free via quests on April 5). This is the last chance to earn Candy Eggs and all previously released pets remain available until April 10.

What's New in Week 4

Sugarfest Week 4 — the final content wave of Sugar Festival 2026 — dropped on April 4, 2026 at 11:15am ET. Here's everything that launched:

  • 3 new limited pets: Chocolate Bunny (Legendary), Pupcake (Ultra-Rare), Easter Bunny (Legendary)
  • Easter Bunny unlocks via quests starting April 5 — completely free
  • Ongoing from Weeks 1–3: Game of Sweets board, all previously released pets, Candy Cliffs, Jerboa Jam, and Waffle Scuffle still active until April 10
⏰ Final Week — Event Ends April 10

Week 4 is the last update in Sugarfest 2026. All event pets — including Candicorn, Mochi Meow, Waffle Wyrm, Latte Kitsune, Jiggly Jerboa, Chocolate Bunny, Pupcake, and Easter Bunny — disappear from in-game earning after April 10. A leftover wagon stays open until April 10 for remaining purchases.

🐇 Chocolate Bunny — Legendary Pet

The Chocolate Bunny is a limited Legendary pet available for 800 Robux (~$10 USD) in the Robux shop starting April 4. It's an Easter-themed Legendary with strong visual appeal — chocolate-brown with a pastel aesthetic that fits the Sugarfest event perfectly.

How to Get the Chocolate Bunny

The Chocolate Bunny is a direct Robux purchase — no Candy Egg grind required. Head to the event shop after the April 4 update and buy it for 800 Robux. It will be available until April 10, 2026, after which it becomes trade-only.

🔥 Should You Buy the Chocolate Bunny?

At 800 Robux (~$10), the Chocolate Bunny is a mid-range Legendary — more accessible than Candicorn (1,000 Robux) but pricier than Latte Kitsune (600 Robux). Easter-themed Legendaries historically hold value well post-event due to strong demand each spring. If you like the design, this is a solid hold.

🎂 Pupcake — Ultra-Rare Pet

The Pupcake is a limited Ultra-Rare pet priced at 50,000 Candy Eggs. It's a cupcake-dog hybrid with playful event-themed styling. Ultra-Rare limited pets often outperform expectations in Neon form post-event due to lower supply relative to Commons.

How to Get the Pupcake

Earn Candy Eggs through all active event activities — the Jerboa Jam minigame, Game of Sweets board, Waffle Scuffle, and pet need bonuses — then spend 50,000 in the event shop. With consistent grinding this is achievable in 2–3 days of active play.

🐰 Easter Bunny — Free Legendary

The Easter Bunny is a completely free Legendary pet unlocked via event quests starting April 5. This is extremely rare for a Legendary — it means higher supply than paid Legendaries but also much higher accessibility for free-to-play players.

How to Get the Easter Bunny

Log in on April 5 and complete the Easter Bunny quest chain that becomes available after the April 5 update. Here's how the unlock flow works:

  1. Log In on April 5 The Easter Bunny quest chain goes live with the April 5 patch. Log in after the update drops and look for the Easter Bunny quest icon near the event area on Adoption Island.
  2. Accept the Quest Chain Talk to the quest NPC (or interact with the Easter Bunny portal) to accept the chain. Full quest requirements will be detailed in this guide within hours of the April 5 update going live.
  3. Complete All Quest Steps The chain consists of multiple tasks — typically collecting items, feeding pets, or playing minigames. Complete each step in sequence to progress.
  4. Claim Your Easter Bunny After completing the final quest, the Easter Bunny is automatically added to your pet collection. No Robux or Candy Eggs required.
💡 Free Legendary Tip

Even though the Easter Bunny is free, don't skip it. Free Legendaries are rare events in Adopt Me. They have slightly lower trading value than paid Legendaries (more supply), but they're still Legendaries and will be unobtainable in-game after April 10. The Neon and Mega Neon forms still carry strong value due to the effort required.

💰 Final Week Candy Egg Priority

With the event ending April 10, here's how to prioritize your remaining Candy Eggs across all four weeks of content:

  1. Top Priority: Mochi Meow (if you haven't yet) — 125,000 Candy Eggs for the highest-effort Legendary of the event. Highest long-term value of all event pets that require grinding.
  2. Second Priority: Pupcake — At 50,000 eggs, this is the best Ultra-Rare of the final week. Strong post-event value in Neon form.
  3. Third Priority: Jiggly Jerboa (multiple for Neon) — At just 6,000 eggs each, the best egg-per-value deal in the event. Grab 4+ for a Neon if you have eggs left over.
  4. Fourth Priority: Waffle Wyrm, cosmetics — If you haven't grabbed the Waffle Wyrm (80K eggs) yet, this is your last chance. Cosmetics like the Sour Glider (60K) also hold value as unique event wearables.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Chocolate Bunny worth 800 Robux?

It depends on your goals. Easter-themed Legendaries in Adopt Me historically hold strong demand post-event, especially from players who missed out. At 800 Robux (~$10), it's a moderate investment. If you enjoy the design and want a limited Legendary, it's a reasonable buy.

Is the Easter Bunny good even though it's free?

Yes — it's still a Legendary, and it will be trade-only after April 10. Free Legendaries typically have slightly more supply than Robux Legendaries (making the Normal form somewhat less valuable), but the Neon and Mega Neon forms remain strong due to the effort required to make them.

Should I grind for Pupcake or Mochi Meow first?

Mochi Meow is the higher-value target at 125K eggs, but Pupcake (50K eggs) is more achievable in the time remaining. If you have enough days left and can grind consistently, prioritize Mochi Meow first. If you're short on time, Pupcake is a better sure-thing.

What happens to my Candy Eggs after April 10?

All event pets become unobtainable after April 10. A vendor wagon may remain briefly but all major event items (including pets) close at the April 10 deadline. Make sure you've spent your eggs on everything you want before then.

Can I still get Week 1, 2, and 3 pets?

Yes — all Sugarfest pets from every week remain available until April 10. Candicorn, Mochi Meow, Waffle Wyrm, Latte Kitsune, and Jiggly Jerboa are all still earnable. This is your last chance for any you missed.
