Everything You Need to Know About Neon Pets

What is a Neon pet?

A Neon pet is created by fusing 4 fully-grown adults of the same species. Neon pets glow brightly with neon colors and are worth significantly more than regular pets. FR Neons (made from 4 FR pets) are worth the most.

What is a Mega Neon?

A Mega Neon is made by fusing 4 Neon pets of the same species. This requires 16 total regular pets. Mega Neons cycle through all neon colors in a rainbow effect and are extremely valuable.

FR vs Normal Neon

Using FR pets to make a Neon creates an "FR Neon" which is worth more than a Normal Neon. The difference in value depends on the pet tier — for lower-rarity pets, FR Neons can be worth 3-5× more than Normal Neons.
