When making usually wins

Lower-tier pets and strong demand pets can justify the aging effort if the finished Neon or Mega commands a real premium over the parts.

When buying usually wins

High-tier pets often have enough collector complexity that it is cheaper to buy the finished result than to gather four or sixteen clean components yourself.
