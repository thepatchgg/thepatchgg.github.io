## First setup

1. Open Google Search Console for `https://thepatchgg.github.io/`
2. Submit the sitemap:

## Weekly routine

1. Publish the new page
2. Add it to the homepage or a relevant guide hub
3. Add it to the sitemap
4. Request indexing in Search Console
5. Check impressions and queries after a few days

## What to watch

- Page indexing
- Search impressions
- CTR
- Queries by page
- Pages that are indexed but getting no clicks yet
