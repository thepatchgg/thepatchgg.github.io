    What EV means here

    This is not full hatch value. It only counts pets that already exist in The Patch benchmark value system, so it works best as a directional comparison instead of a final verdict.

    Why some eggs look weak

    An egg can still be interesting even if the benchmark EV is low. It may just be full of pets that are not yet part of the shared benchmark ladder.

    Best next step

    Use this tool to find high-signal eggs, then open the benchmark pet pages or the trade calculator if you want to compare the likely prize against other trade targets.
