Board beta

The trading board is being rebuilt the honest way.

The old board looked live, but its submission flow and status handling were not strong enough to earn trust. This version is intentionally labeled as a moderated beta while the posting workflow is rebuilt.

Check trade value first Report a board issue

Beta rules

  • No fake "active trader" countersFixed
  • No silent `no-cors` submission black boxFixed
  • Listings shown here are sample moderated formatsBeta

What is available now

You can use the value list, trade calculator, and Neon calculator to price a deal before you ever message another trader.

What is coming back later

Public posting, moderation queues, abuse reporting, and clear listing status will return after the board backend is rebuilt.

Example listing format

These sample cards show the standard we want the board to meet once live submissions reopen.

Sample listing Want to Trade

Bat Dragon for high-tier offers

Offering: FR Bat Dragon. Looking for: Shadow Dragon plus adds, or equivalent high-tier bundle.

Status should eventually show: posted time, moderation status, and whether the trader is still active.

Sample listing Looking For

Searching for Cow or Turtle upgrades

Offering: mix of mid-tier adds. Looking for: Cow, Turtle, or similar liquid pet with stable demand.

Status should eventually show: trade lane, benchmark context, and a clear stale-listing timeout.

Sample listing Offer review

Neon builder bundle

Offering: four clean components and looking for a direct Neon comparison to save aging time.

Status should eventually show: whether the listing is for parts, finished Neon, or Mega planning.

Safer workflow until the board returns

Use the trade calculator to benchmark the deal, compare Neon math if aging is involved, then only move into direct player negotiation once the numbers make sense.

Why the board is paused

The old version had unreliable submission confirmation and credibility issues. Pausing it publicly is better than pretending it is production-ready.

The trading board is in moderated beta while submission and abuse-reporting tools are rebuilt.
