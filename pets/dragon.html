How strong is Dragon in trades right now?

Dragon currently carries low demand with medium confidence. The current signal is falling, so it works best as a entry tier comparison page rather than a guaranteed server price.

Why does no-potion Dragon have a different value?

Right now, no-potion Dragon sits at 0.4 versus 1.1 for the default version, so the clean copy is slightly softer by about 0.7 in this lane.
