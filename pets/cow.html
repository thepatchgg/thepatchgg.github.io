Cow FAQ

Is Cow a strong benchmark pet right now?

Cow currently carries high demand with high confidence. The current signal is rising, so it works best as a mid tier reference point rather than a guaranteed server price.

Why does no-potion Cow have a different value?

Right now, no-potion Cow sits at 28.5 versus 27 for the default version, so collectors are paying about 1.5 more for the cleaner lane.

Which pets should I compare with Cow?

The best sanity checks right now are Turtle, Dalmatian, and Hedgehog. Those pages sit close to Cow in real trade conversations and help you spot whether a server is drifting too far from broader benchmark behavior.
